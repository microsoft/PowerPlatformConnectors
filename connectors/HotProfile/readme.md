
## Your Connector Name
HotProfile


## Pre-requisites
You must subscribe to HotProfile to use this connector.
https://www.hammock.jp/hpr/

## API documentation
You must subscribe to HotProfile to use this connector.

## Supported Operations
The following actions are set.
(There is no trigger.)

・ Get business cards
・ Register business card
・ Update business card
・ Acquire leads
・ Register a lead
・ Update a lead
・ Acquire company
・ Register a company
・ Update the company
